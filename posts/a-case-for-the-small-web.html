The Answer Engine

AI-generated content on Medium surged from 1.8% in 2022 to 37% in 2024. Similarly, quora saw an increase from 2 to 39% during the same period.

we aren't just living alongside ai anymore. we're living through it.

The conventional narrative frames this as competition--AI vs. human creators, chatbots vs. search engines, automation vs. authenticity. But the real story here is more troubling: AI isn't just creating content, it's becoming the intermediary that controls what information we consume. In the new landscape, independent websites and "The Small Web" This term was coined by Ben Hoyt aren't just nice to have, they're as relevant as ever for preserving human connection and discovery in an increasingly abstracted world.

the mechanism is subtle but systematic. ai doesn't simply compete with human-created content, it intercepts and repackages it, keeping users within controlled ecosystems.
